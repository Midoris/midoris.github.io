Loan Tracker: You Owe Me

AI Follow-Ups & Interest Rate

Stop losing money over forgotten loans — and stop stressing about what to say when it’s time to get paid back.

YouOweMe is a loan tracker, IOU, and debt app for friends, couples, families, roommates — and small businesses who want to track shared expenses, client payments, and repayments without awkward conversations. Log what you borrow or lend, split bills, and send polite payment reminders — with AI help when you don’t want to “word it wrong.”

Key features

AI Follow-Up Writing (AI Follow-Ups)

Select a person and a tone, and YouOweMe generates a ready-to-send follow-up message on your behalf. It includes a clear breakdown of what’s owed and can include a secure summary link — the other person can view it without installing the app.

Voice-to-Entry (Add loans without typing)

Just speak naturally: “I lent Alex 30 euros for groceries yesterday.” YouOweMe automatically creates the correct entry with names, amounts, categories, and dates.

Smart Payment Reminders

Set reminders for payments you need to collect or repay. Great for one-time IOUs, payback dates, and recurring bills.

FAQ

What is YouOweMe?

YouOweMe is a loan tracker and IOU app that helps you track debts, shared expenses, and repayments — so you always know who owes whom and how much.

Is it good for tracking money between friends or roommates?

Yes. It’s designed for shared expenses, splitting bills, and keeping a clear running balance between people.

Can it help me ask someone to pay me back politely?

Yes. AI Follow-Ups generates a ready-to-send message with a clear breakdown of what’s owed.

Does it support recurring bills?

Yes. Use recurring entries and reminders for rent, subscriptions, utilities, and repeating payments.
